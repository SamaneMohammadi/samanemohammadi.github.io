About

I'm a PhD candidate at the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh, advised by Dr. Marc Juarez. I work on efficient and trustworthy federated learning — building distributed machine-learning systems that stay private, fair, and practical to run on the kind of resource-constrained edge hardware you actually find outside the lab.

Before Edinburgh I spent four years as an industrial PhD researcher at RISE Research Institutes of Sweden, working across two EU projects (DAIS and DADAP) with academic, industrial, and clinical partners. These days I'm especially drawn to fair, efficient fine-tuning of large language models with LoRA.
