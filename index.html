About

I'm in the final year of my PhD in the School of Informatics at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Dr. Marc Juarez. I completed my M.Sc. in Information Technology Engineering at the University of Tehran.

My research centers on Efficient and Trustworthy federated learning (FL) — distributed machine-learning systems that stay private, fair, and practical to run on the resource-constrained edge hardware you find outside the lab.
